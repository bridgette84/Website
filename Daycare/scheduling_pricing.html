How to schedule an appointment and pricing for our daycare

If you are looking for a quality and affordable at home daycare for your child, you have come to the right place. We offer a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for children from 6 months to 5 years old. Our daycare is licensed, insured and certified by the state. We have a low child-to-staff ratio, a spacious indoor and outdoor play area, and a curriculum that promotes the physical, social, emotional and cognitive development of your child.

To schedule an appointment with us, you can call us at (888) 234-7755 or email us at contact@homesweethome.net.
